## Registry几个名词
| 名词 | 层级 | 理解 |
| :---: |:---:| :---:|
| Registry | Top level | Overall Administration |
| Registrar | Middleman | Accredited organization that registers into a registry |
| Registrant | Consumer | Owns the Right to register |
