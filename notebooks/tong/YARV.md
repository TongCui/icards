## YARV

- Yet Another Ruby VM
- a bytecode interpreter that was developed for the Ruby programming language by Koichi Sasada. 
- intruduced by 1.9
- also named KRI (Koichi's Ruby Interpreter)
- the original Ruby MRI, Yukihiro Matsumoto.
